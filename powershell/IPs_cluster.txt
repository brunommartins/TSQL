
Ver IPs locais via PowerShell (em todos os nós):
Get-NetIPAddress -AddressFamily IPv4 | Where-Object {$_.IPAddress -like "10.*"} | Select-Object IPAddress, InterfaceAlias


Ver interfaces visíveis ao cluster:
Get-ClusterNetworkInterface | Where-Object {$_.State -eq "Up"} | Select-Object NodeName, Network, InterfaceName, Address


Ver sub-rede associada ao IP Cluster:
Get-ClusterResource "PSQLMIRDB_AG_10.157.188.13" | Get-ClusterParameter | Where-Object {$_.Name -eq "Address"}


Get-NetAdapterAdvancedProperty -DisplayName 'Jumbo Packet'
Get-NetAdapterAdvancedProperty -DisplayName 'Small Rx Buffers'
Get-NetAdapterAdvancedProperty -DisplayName 'JRx Ring #1 Size'
